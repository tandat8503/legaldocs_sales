ยง 8-206. COMPLETION OR ALTERATION OF SECURITY CERTIFICATE.
(a) If a
security certificate
contains the signatures necessary to its issue or transfer but is incomplete in any other respect:
(1) any person may complete it by filling in the blanks as authorized; and
(2) even if the blanks are incorrectly filled in, the security certificate as completed is enforceable by a purchaser who took it for value and without notice of the incorrectness.
(b) A complete
security certificate
that has been improperly altered, even if fraudulently, remains enforceable, but only according to its original terms.
