ยง 8-508. DUTY OF SECURITIES INTERMEDIARY TO CHANGE ENTITLEMENT HOLDER's POSITION TO OTHER FORM OF SECURITY HOLDING.
A
securities intermediary
shall act at the direction of an
entitlement holder
to change a
security entitlement
into another available form of holding for which the entitlement holder is eligible, or to cause the
financial asset
to be transferred to a
securities account
of the entitlement holder with another securities intermediary. A securities intermediary satisfies the duty if:
(1) the securities intermediary acts as agreed upon by the
entitlement holder
and the securities intermediary; or
(2) in the absence of agreement, the securities intermediary exercises due care in accordance with reasonable commercial standards to follow the direction of the entitlement holder.
