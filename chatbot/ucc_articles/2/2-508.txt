ยง 2-508. Cure by Seller of Improper Tender or Delivery; Replacement.
(1) Where any tender or delivery by the
seller
is rejected because non-conforming and the time for performance has not yet expired, the seller may seasonably notify the
buyer
of his intention to cure and may then within the
contract
time make a
conforming
delivery.
(2) Where the
buyer
rejects a non-conforming tender which the
seller
had reasonable grounds to believe would be acceptable with or without money allowance the seller may if he seasonably notifies the buyer have a further reasonable time to substitute a
conforming
tender.
