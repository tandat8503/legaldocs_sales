ยง 4A-503. INJUNCTION	OR RESTRAINING ORDER WITH RESPECT TO FUNDS TRANSFER.

For proper cause and in compliance with applicable law,
	a court may restrain (i) a person from issuing a
payment
	order
to initiate a
funds transfer
, (ii) an
originator's
	bank
from executing the payment order of the
originator
,
	or (iii) the
beneficiary's bank
from releasing
	funds to the
beneficiary
or the beneficiary from
	withdrawing the funds. A court may not otherwise restrain a person from
	issuing a payment order, paying or receiving payment of a payment order, or
	otherwise acting with respect to a funds transfer.
