		payment order.

"

		bank

receiving bank
other
		than the
originator's bank
or the
beneficiary's
		bank
.
(c)
"
Originator
" means
	the
sender
of the first
payment
	order
in a
funds transfer
.
(d)
"
Originator's
		bank
" means (i) the
receiving bank
to
		which the
payment order
of the
originator
is
		issued if the originator is not a
bank
, or (ii) the originator
		if the originator is a bank.
