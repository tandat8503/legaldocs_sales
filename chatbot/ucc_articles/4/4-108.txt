ยง 4-108. TIME OF RECEIPT OF ITEMS.

(a) For the purpose of allowing time to process
items
, prove balances, and make the necessary entries on its books to determine its position for the day, a
bank
may fix an
afternoon
hour of 2 P.M. or later as a cutoff hour for the handling of money and items and the making of entries on its books.
(b) An
item
or deposit of money received on any day after a cutoff hour so fixed or after the close of the
banking day
may be treated as being received at the opening of the next banking day.
