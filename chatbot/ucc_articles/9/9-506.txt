ยง 9-506. EFFECT OF ERRORS OR OMISSIONS.
(a) [Minor errors and omissions.]
A
financing statement
substantially
	satisfying the requirements of this part is effective, even if it has minor
	errors or omissions, unless the errors or omissions make the financing statement
	seriously misleading.
(b) [Financing statement seriously
	misleading.]
Except as otherwise provided in subsection (c), a
financing
		statement
that fails sufficiently to provide the name of the
debtor
in
		accordance with Section
9-503(a)
is seriously misleading.
(c) [Financing statement not seriously
	misleading.]
If a search of the records of the
filing
		office
under the
debtor
's correct name, using the
		filing office's standard search logic, if any, would disclose a
financing
		statement
that fails sufficiently to provide the name of the debtor in
		accordance with Section
9-503(a)
, the name provided
		does not make the financing statement seriously misleading.
(d) ["Debtor's correct name."]
For purposes of Section
9-508(b)
,
	the "debtor's correct name" in subsection (c) means the correct name
	of the
new debtor
.
