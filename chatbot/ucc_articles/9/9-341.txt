§ 9-341. BANK’S RIGHTS AND DUTIES WITH RESPECT TO DEPOSIT ACCOUNT.
Except as otherwise provided in Section
9-340(c)
,
	and unless the
bank
otherwise agrees in an
authenticated
record
,
	a bank's rights and duties with respect to a
deposit
	account
maintained with the bank are not terminated, suspended, or modified
	by:
(1) the creation, attachment, or perfection of a security
	interest in the
deposit account
;
(2) the bank's knowledge of the security interest; or
(3) the bank's receipt of instructions from the
secured
		party
.
